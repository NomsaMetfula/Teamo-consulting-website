About Teamo Consulting

Teamo Consulting is a 100% Black woman-owned consultancy dedicated to developing skilled, confident and employable individuals while helping organisations build strong, agile and high-performing teams.

We bridge the gap between education and employment through structured internship and learnership programmes, leadership development, and B-BBEE aligned training solutions.

Our purpose is simple: Transform lives through people development.

Ms Nene

Founder & Lead Consultant

With over 15 years in HR, talent development and youth empowerment, Ms Nene is passionate about creating sustainable career pathways for South African youth.
